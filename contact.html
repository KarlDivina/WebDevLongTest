Contacting Apple

Apple Online Store

Apple.com/ph is a convenient place to purchase
Apple products and accessories from Apple. You can
buy online, or call 1800/1651/0525 (Smart/PLDT),
1800/8474/7382 (Globe).

You can get information about an order you placed
on the Apple Online Store through the Order Status page.
If you prefer, you can also get order status or
make changes by phone at 1800-16510525
(Smart/PLDT), 1800-8474-7382 (Globe).

Visit Apple Online Store Help on details about
purchasing, shipping, checking order status, returns,
and more.

How to Buy for Education

If you are a student or teacher, visit the Apple Store for Education
for Education or call 1800‑1651‑0525 (Smart/PLDT),
1800‑8474‑7382 (Globe).

If you are buying on behalf of an educational
institution, find an Apple Authorized Reseller, who
can also provide ongoing support and service based
on your school’s needs.

Find Authorized Resellers

Use our Reseller Locator to find an Apple Authorized

multi-platform services, and Mac-based solutions for
a wide variety of organizations.
